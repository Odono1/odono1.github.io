Meet the Team

Simon Madden

Simon Madden's mastery of the lens is matched only by his innovative approach to marketing, honed through his studies in Marketing, Innovation, and Technology at DCU. A native of the verdant Tipperary landscape, Simon's photographic journey is a testament to his passion for the natural world and his commitment to capturing its ephemeral beauty. With over a decade of experience, Simon has seamlessly blended his academic insights with his artistic endeavors, utilizing drone technology to elevate his photographic narrative, offering a fresh perspective that resonates with audiences far and wide.

Joseph O'Donnachadha

Joseph O'Donnachadha, a skilled chronicler of Louth's rich tapestry, brings an academic background in Marketing, Innovation, and Technology from DCU to his photographic ventures. His lens is his quill, writing visual stories of the urban heartbeat, where history's whispers meet the modern bustle. Joseph's dedication to capturing life's diverse scenes is fueled by his innovative marketing strategies, enabling him to showcase the essence of Louth through a captivating, story-driven lens that engages and inspires.

Finn Kerins

In Kildare, where equine majesty meets the camera's gaze, Finn Kerins stands out as a photographer with a unique blend of visceral energy and academic prowess. A student of Marketing, Innovation, and Technology at DCU, Finn's approach to photography is infused with a marketer's insight, crafting images that speak to both the heart and the mind. His photos, a tableau of life's unscripted moments, are imbued with the movement and passion of the scenes he captures. Finn's academic journey complements his artistic vision, allowing him to create a portfolio that is as compelling as it is authentic.
